## Performance Tips

### Model Selection by Device:

- **Low-end** (2 GB RAM): SmolLM2 360M (119 MB)
- **Mid-range** (3 GB RAM): Qwen 0.5B (374 MB)
- **High-end** (4+ GB RAM): Llama 3.2 1B (815 MB) or Qwen 1.5B (1.2 GB)

### Speed Optimization:
